Guns

Know your capabilities

"Master Every Weapon, Dominate Every Round"

Welcome to your ultimate Valorant gun guide. Whether you’re learning the basics or perfecting aim, this manual equips you with the game’s diverse firearm selection — sidearms, SMGs, rifles, and snipers. Discover damage stats, recoil patterns, and tactics that give you the upper hand every play.

Valorant isn’t just about aim — it’s about making the right choice at the right time. With this weapon knowledge, you’ll never waste creds, whiffs, or wins again.

Pistols

Classic

The free sidearm. Versatile with a primary fire for precision and burst-fire alternate for close combat.

Frenzy

A pistol with high fire rate and short range — deadly when rushing tight spaces.

Ghost

Silenced and accurate. A go-to pick for eco rounds and long-range pistol duels.

Sheriff

High damage and powerful recoil. Perfect for players who trust their headshots.

Sheriff

A pocket shotgun that dominates at point-blank range. Extremely cheap but limited by low ammo and range.

SMGs

Stinger

Fast and fierce. Great for close-quarters combat.

Spectre

Silenced, mobile, and reliable — ideal for eco rounds and mid-range engagements.

Shotguns

Bucky

Close-range beast. Devastating power but limited reach.

Judge

Fully automatic shotgun with brutal firepower for rushes and choke points.

Assault Rifles

Bulldog

Versatile and cost-effective rifle. Great for players on a budget.

Guardian

Precise and powerful single-shot rifle for patient marksmen.

Phantom

Silenced, accurate, and deadly at short to mid-range.

Phantom

The rival to the Phantom. Fully automatic with no damage fall-off - one headshot is lethal at any distance.

Sniper Rifles

Marshal

Light sniper. One-shot headshots for skilled aimers.

Operator

The most powerful rifle in Valorant. One body shot, one kill.

Machine Guns

Ares

The cheaper LMG, offering solid suppressive fire and high magazine capacity.

Odin

The heavy powerhouse. Massive fire rate and damage, perfect for locking down sites.
