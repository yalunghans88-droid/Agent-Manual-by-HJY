Agent Roles in Valorant: Master the Meta

Valorant agents are grouped into four core roles, each defined by unique responsibilities and designed to create balanced team compositions. Here's how each role functions:

Duelist

The Aggressive Scorers
Role: Self-reliant fraggers who lead offensives, securing openings for their team.
Playstyle: Fast-paced and high-risk.
Notables: Jett, Phoenix, Neon, Raze, Reyna, Yoru, Iso.

Controller

The Strategic Enforcers
Role: Manipulate battlefield control by blocking vision, shaping space, and dictating engagements.
Playstyle: Tactical and map-centric.
Notables: Brimstone, Omen, Viper, Astra, Harbor, Clove.

Initiator

The Entry Enablers
Role: Break enemy positions, gather intel, and facilitate entry for teammates.
Playstyle: Disruptive and support-focused.
Notables: Sova, Breach, Skye, KAY/O, Fade, Gekko.

Sentinels

The Defensive Anchors
Role: Hold ground, fortify sites, and offer team support through healing or revives.
Playstyle: Defensive and protection-centric.
Notables: Sage, Cypher, Killjoy, Chamber, Deadlock, Vyse.
