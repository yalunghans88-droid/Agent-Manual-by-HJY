Agent Roles in Valorant: Master the Meta

Valorant’s agents are grouped into four primary roles. Each role is designed to enhance team coordination and strategy. Understanding each role helps players maximize their potential and adapt effectively in matches.

Duelist

Duelists specialize in aggressive play and are the front-liners in every engagement. Their self-sufficient combat skills make them ideal for leading attacks and securing kills.

  • Agents: Jett, Phoenix, Reyna, Raze, Yoru, Iso

Controller

Controllers are experts in map control. They provide cover, block vision, and manipulate the battlefield to help their team advance strategically.

  • Agents: Omen, Brimstone, Viper, Astra, Harbor, Clove

Initiator

Initiators open paths for their teams. They disrupt enemy defenses, gather intel, and initiate attacks through crowd control or reconnaissance abilities.

  • Agents: Sova, Breach, KAY/O, Skye, Fade, Gekko

Sentinels

Sentinels are defensive anchors. They excel at locking down areas, protecting teammates, and providing intel to maintain map control and prevent flanks.

  • Agents: Sage, Killjoy, Cypher, Chamber, Deadlock
