Tips & Strategies

Know your capabilities

Want to level up your Valorant skills? Whether you’re a new player or climbing the competitive ladder, our Tips & Strategies will help you improve your aim, master your agents, and dominate every map. Let’s turn you into a Radiant-level player!

Beginner Tips

  • Always aim for the head – practice your crosshair placement at head level.
  • Walk, don’t run when peeking corners to stay silent.
  • Manage your economy – don’t buy every round, learn when to save and when to force buy.
  • Learn one agent first before switching to others.
  • Use custom games or The Range to improve aim and recoil control.

Advanced Strategies

  • Use fake rotations to confuse the enemy team.
  • Always trade kills – never push alone if possible.
  • Master post-plant positioning – play around bomb timings.
  • Use abilities smartly – don’t waste flashes, smokes, or walls without a plan.
  • Communicate with your team for better synergy and executes.

Agent Synergies

Valorant is not just about aim – it’s about teamwork. Some agents work better together:

  • Breach + Raze: Flash + Grenade combo for free kills.
  • Omen + Jett: Smokes + Dash for fast entry plays.
  • Sova + Brimstone: Recon + Orbital Strike for post-plant wins.

Map Control

  • Knowing the maps is as important as aiming.
  • Memorize callouts – it helps your team coordinate.
  • Learn common hiding spots and spike plant positions.
  • Control choke points early to block enemy pushes.
  • Use your utility to gain space and deny enemy rotations.
